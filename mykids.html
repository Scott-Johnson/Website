MY KIDS

A Film By Luke Harris

A modest living room.
A perfect family image.

But some secrets are too heavy to carry, and some truths refuse to stay buried in the dark.

The Vision

Written and Directed by Luke Harris, *MY KIDS* is a gripping exploration of faith, image, and the devastating cost of unconditional protection. A modern drama that peels back the layers of a grieving southern family to reveal the terrifying lengths we go to hide our sins.
