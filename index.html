ABOUT SCOTT

Scott pursued his undergraduate education at the esteemed USC School of Cinematic Arts, specializing in Film & Television Production—widely regarded as the world's leading academic program in the film & entertainment field.

While at USC, Scott made history scoring over 100 USC thesis films and earned the reputation of being a “secret weapon” within the program for his work ethic and collaborative spirit. His contributions have been instrumental in numerous projects that achieved critical acclaim, including those that have garnered awards at the Cannes Film Festival and the Student Academy Awards.

In 2020, Scott's work was showcased on a global stage when he scored a “Shot on iPhone” short for Apple Inc.

Prior to graduating film school, Scott was invited to join Hans Zimmer’s Bleeding Fingers Music.

Scott's career trajectory has seen him collaborate with industry luminaries such as Roeban Katz and Alexandre Desplat. He has also worked with major networks like ABC, where he contributed to special editions of ABC 20/20’s “Black Panther: In Search of Wakanda” and “Disney 100: A Century of Dreams”.

Scott's versatility extends beyond scoring, having been commissioned to sound design segments for Coachella.
